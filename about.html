Process

How I usually work

  1. Map the task.What is the user trying to decide, compare, or complete?
  2. Find the friction.Where does the flow force extra clicks, memory work, or uncertainty?
  3. Restructure the screen.Group actions by intent, not by what looks convenient on the canvas.
  4. Design the states.Empty, loading, error, confirmation, destructive actions, and after-action screens.
  5. Prototype and refine.Test whether the next step is obvious before adding more polish.
